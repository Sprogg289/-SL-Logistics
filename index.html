About SL Logistics

On the 12th of October, 2024 — [SL] Logistics was founded by Sprogg28 with a mission to build an inclusive community of drivers from around the world who share a passion for exploring the vast roads of Europe and America in their favorite driving games.

Through [SL] Logistics organized convoys and events, the company Is aiming to unite drivers in their shared passion for exploration, bridging all the distances and building lasting friendships along the way.

With all of our staff and community We all can grow togeather as a Community.
